PRD: AI Semiconductor Sourcing Chatbot – Loading Experience 1. Objective Design a high-trust, visually immersive loading screen for an AI chatbot powered by OpenAI that assists large technology companies in global semiconductor supplier sourcing. 2. Design Strategy Core Intent: Transform “waiting” into perceived active processing. Emotional Targets: Confidence (enterprise-grade), Sophistication (AI-driven), Control. 3. Visual System Background: #03170F (deep green-black gradient) Primary Accent: #5AEB56 (neon intelligence signal) Secondary Accent: #098F3B Highlight: #3CF2FF (soft cyan data contrast) Atmosphere: Central green beam, particle field, pulsing glow. 4. Layout Structure - Minimal Top Nav: Logo (Procurement Pilot) + "Connected to OpenAI" status. - Main Loading Core: - Headline: “Sourcing Global Semiconductor Suppliers” - Subtext: “Analyzing suppliers, pricing signals, and logistics across 40+ markets” - Visualization Panel: Glassmorphism card, neon border glow, live metrics (Suppliers Indexed, Regions Scanned, etc.), animated line graph, circular progress (Match Optimization). - Progress Feed: Step-by-step AI reasoning (Indexing, Normalizing, Matching, etc.). - Background: Wireframe globe with glowing nodes. 5. Motion: Glow pulse, data streams, fluctuating graph, smooth progress ring, sliding step transitions. 6. Microcopy: Analytical, enterprise-grade, precise. 7. UX: Reduce perceived wait time through constant movement and trust signals.
